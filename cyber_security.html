Subject Information

Cyber Security is the practice of protecting computers, networks, systems, and data from digital attacks, unauthorized access, and damage. With the increasing use of the internet and digital technologies, cyber threats such as hacking, malware, phishing, and data breaches have become more common. Cyber security helps ensure that sensitive information remains safe and that digital systems operate securely.
