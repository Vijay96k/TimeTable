Subject Information

Mathematical Aptitude is a subject that focuses on developing a person’s ability to understand, analyze, and solve numerical and logical problems efficiently. It involves the application of basic mathematical concepts such as arithmetic, algebra, percentages, ratios, and data interpretation to solve real-life and problem-based questions.
