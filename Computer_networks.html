Subject Information

A Computer Network is a system in which two or more computers and devices are connected to share information, resources, and services. These devices communicate with each other through wired or wireless communication channels using standard protocols.

  • Types of Computer Networks (LAN, MAN, WAN)
  • Network Models (OSI Model and TCP/IP Model)
  • Network Devices (Router, Switch, Hub, Modem, etc.)
  • Network Protocols
